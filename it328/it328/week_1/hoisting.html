Hoisting is something that is not that difficult to understand which is the runtime interrupter raises the declaration of variables to the top of the current scope as if the writer had put them there. Simple and straight forward, what is puzzling and difficult to understand reasons behind the design, as well as less-than-obvious changes in code logic when hoisting occurs.
