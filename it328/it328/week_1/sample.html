Star Trek: TOS (1966 - 1969)
Wagon Train to the Stars. A western and Sci-fi combined together. In 1966 Westerns were very popular on national TV, and race to the moon was a national obsession. Star Trek was pitched to NBC as being a action adventure Western in space. CBS has turned down the show already in favor the Lost In Space that premiered the same year.
