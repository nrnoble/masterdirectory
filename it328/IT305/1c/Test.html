Scrambled Green Eggs and Ham

TO: For Mr. Hand
FROM: Jeff Specoli

Green eggs are one of favorite foods for delicious rich scramble green eggs and ham

Ingredients

  • green eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
  • 1 HUGE BUD!!

Method

  1. Smoke the bud!
  2. Melt butter in frying pan over medium heat
  3. Gently mix the green eggs, ham, and cream in a bowl (the kind of bowl you cook, not smoke)
  4. Once butter had melted, add cream, green eggs, ham.
  5. Using that spatula thing fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 second (as if you making an omelet)
  6. When the eggs are still moist remove from heat (it will continue to cook on plate until served)
